Common Questions

Everything you need to know about our process, pricing, and app publishing.

How long does it take to build an app?

+

A standard MVP (Minimum Viable Product) usually takes 2-4 months. Complex enterprise solutions with custom backends can take 6-12 months depending on the feature list.

Do you help with App Store and Google Play publishing?

+

Yes, we handle the entire process: from preparing metadata and screenshots to passing the review. We can publish apps to your own developer account or host them on our corporate accounts.

Who owns the source code?

+

You do. Upon final payment, we transfer 100% of the intellectual property rights and source code to you. This is strictly defined in our legal agreement.

Do you provide post-launch support?

+

Absolutely. We offer various maintenance packages to ensure your app stays compatible with new OS versions (iOS/Android updates) and remains secure.
